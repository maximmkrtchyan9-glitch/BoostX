The Colosseum Rules

1. General provisions

1.1. These Regulations define the procedure and conditions of the contest (hereinafter referred to as the Contest).

1.2. The organizer of the Contest is Boost (X) (hereinafter referred to as the Organizer).

2. Terms of participation

2.1. To participate, you must fill out an application for the participation of the token in the voting:

  • Specify the correct ticker and contract address;
  • Upload the project's logo and banner;
  • Fill in the project description;
  • Provide a valid contact for communication: Telegram or Twitter;
  • Wait for the token to be approved for participation: Share a link to vote.

2.2. Token Admission Criteria:

  • The token must be launched on the mainnet;
  • The token must have liquidity on one of the exchanges or DEX;
  • The token should not have any risk indicators for users.

2.3. The Organizer reserves the right to reject the application for participation without explanation in case of violation of these Rules.

3. The order of the Competition

3.1. The competition is held in three stages:

  • Stage 1. Submission of applications: applications are submitted daily with the word getting into the vote on the next calendar day, after the day of submission of the application. Tokens whose applications will be approved by the time of the start of voting will be allowed to vote. The application status can be viewed in the application card.
  • Stage 2. Voting: from [Start Date] to [End Date].
  • Stage 3. Summing up the results: no later than 6 hours after the end of voting.

3.2. All accepted tokens are published in the voting list on the official page of the Contest.

4. Voting

4.1. Voting is open and public.

4.2. Each user has the right to cast one vote for one token they like within 24 hours.

4.3. The use of bots, scripts, and the creation of fake accounts to cheat votes is strictly prohibited.

4.4. The Organizer has the right to cancel votes and disqualify the participant in case of violations.

5. Determining the winners and the prize pool

5.1. The winners are determined based on the rating based on the number of votes.

5.2. Prize pool: [description of bonuses].

6. The phase of working with the winners

6.1. Notification of winners:

The organizer communicates with representatives of the winning projects within two (2) hours after the results are summed up. Communication is carried out exclusively through the Telegram or Twitter accounts specified in the application.

6.2. A prerequisite for receiving a prize:

To confirm the legitimacy of the victory and receive the prize, the official representative of the winning project must, within 3 (three) hours from the date of publication of the results:

  • Post a post on the official Telegram channel or Twitter account of the token, containing a mention of the Organizer's account and a link to the contest page.
  • The text of the publication must be agreed with the Organizer.
  • The publication must remain available for at least 7 (seven) calendar days.

6.3. Consequences of failure to comply with the conditions:

If the representative of the winning project does not get in touch within 5 (five) hours from the moment of the first notification OR refuses to fulfill the conditions of clause 6.2, he loses the right to receive the prize. In this case, the prize goes to the next participant in the ranking.

7. Final provisions

7.1. By participating in the Contest, the user agrees to these Rules and undertakes to provide reliable contact information for communication.
